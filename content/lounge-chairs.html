Why Are Lounge Chairs Important for Interior Design in NZ?

It is no secret that lounge chairs play a major role in ensuring both function and fashion when it comes to indoor interior design in New Zealand. Unlike major renovations, changing the look of a room can be as simple as adding a lounge chair. Lounge chairs can be considered statement pieces since they add aesthetic value and come in different styles, fabrics, and colors to suit any decor. They embody such comfort and ergonomic support, apart from ensuring aesthetics, that they are ideal for reading, relaxation, or social interaction. In unique designs, they can be the focal points of other furniture, ultimately creating a living space that exudes warmth and an inviting appeal.

What is Polyurethane Foam Material in Our Lounge Chairs?

Polyurethane foam (PUF) is widely used in our lounge chairs because of the great comfort, flexibility, and durability it offers. Unlike the harmful isocyanates associated with traditional foams, PUF is a much safer, even more sustainable, alternatives. This innovation extends to the development of bio-based polyols and natural additives, such as rice hulls and coffee chaff, which enhance the strengthening capacity of foams by up to 400% without compromising comfort (Mort et al., 2023).

Moreover, the addition of phosphorus leads to fire-retardant PUFs that enhance safety, whereas the waterborne polyurethane foams, are eco-friendly and depict superior resistance to water moisture (Meda et al., 2021; Saibaba, 2021). Hence, PUF will be an ideal choice for making superior, safe, and sustainable lounge chairs.

How Can I Use the Lounge Chairs to Decorate My House?

There are many styles of lounge chairs, so you can use them to create anything from:

  • Room Centerpiece: Place a lounge chair along with a a 2-3 seater sofa, a coffee table, and a carpet to create a balanced focal point. This is an ideal setting for receiving guests or spending times with the family, with varied seating and a cohesive look.
  • Room Corner: A corner with a lounge chair, a side table, and an added plant or a floor lamp creates an open corner into a reading nook. The side table brings in convenience, while the plant or the lamp adds the warmth and character.
  • Window Area: With two lounge chairs on either side of a coffee table by the window, you immediately get an attractive area that's well-lit. The natural light and the convenience of having a surface to place your drinks on create an inviting space just for morning coffees or chats. These setups can add style to any room and provide comfortable and useful seating at the same time.

How to Set Up Lounge Chairs for Various Living Room Layouts?

Lounge chairs have a way of dictating the setting in your living room. In open-plan areas, they help provide careful definition to different zones-be it a reading corner or a truly relaxing spot that adds much-needed structure to such large catch-all spaces. Small living rooms might use compact lounge chairs to create coziness without feeling overwhelmed by a small space. Meanwhile, in larger rooms, the use of several lounge chairs creates multiple, more private seating areas that extend the style and functionality. You decide on the appropriate placement; your lounge chairs will make both the appearance and functionality of your living room even better.

How to Maintain My Lounge Chair for Longer Lifespan?

Proper maintenance of your lounge chair will extend its life and allow it to look wonderful for years. Often, cleaning of the fabric is done where dust accumulation can be avoided by just a gentle vacuum or upholstery cleaner. Wearing of armrest covers or rotation of cushions from time to time can avoid these wear-and-tear problems. You can also avoid fabric fading and degradation of foam by placing the chair away from direct sunlight and water contents. You can use your lounge chair for many years in comfort and style with these easy care tips.
